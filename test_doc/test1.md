*1. System Overview*
The C-007 model is a high-capacity rotary screw compressor designed for continuous industrial pneumatic applications. It features an advanced thermal regulation system and integrated vibration monitoring.

*2. Predictive Maintenance Baselines*
Historical data indicates that Compressor-7 units typically exhibit predictable wear patterns. Standard operation dictates a major service interval every 12 months. However, if the unit logs 3 failures in the last 12 months, similar equipment failed on average 8 months after the 3rd incident. This threshold should automatically flag the unit for proactive maintenance.

*3. Troubleshooting: Thermal Overload*

* *Symptom*: Unscheduled shutdown due to high discharge temperature.
* *Potential Causes*: Restricted airflow, degraded synthetic coolant, or malfunctioning thermostatic valve.
* *Resolution*: Verify coolant levels. Replace the fluid if operating hours exceed 8,000. Inspect the oil cooler core for particulate blockages.
