### Cascade Failure Incident Log

*Document Type*: Incident Log
*Equipment Name*: Pump A & Pipeline B
*Equipment ID*: P-101 (Pump A), PL-202 (Pipeline B)
*Date of Record*: 2025-11-02

*1. Incident Description*
At 14:30 hours, a catastrophic mechanical failure in Pump A triggered an automated system halt. Because Pump A is critical, this scheduled or unscheduled stop immediately affected downstream processes, specifically halting Pipeline B.

*2. Relationship & Impact Analysis*
The knowledge graph mapping indicates a direct dependency between these units. The sudden loss of pressure from Pump A caused a backflow event in Pipeline B, stressing the check valves.

*3. Root Cause Analysis (RCA)*

* *Primary Failure*: Mechanical binding in Pump A.
* *Secondary Failure*: Cavitation damage to Pipeline B intake valves.
* *Actionable Insight*: Future maintenance on Pump A must include a controlled depressurization of Pipeline B to prevent cascade failures.
